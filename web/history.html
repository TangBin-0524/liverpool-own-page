Liverpool Football Club is founded by John Houlding.

First League Title

Liverpool secured its first Football League champion.

1950~1960s

Liverpool's dynasty

Bill Shankly's arrival at Liverpool on the cusp of the 1960s set the club on course to establish a dynasty.

1976

The King Darglish

Kenny Dalglish: The Scot who ascended the Liverpool FC throne

1985

The Heysel Stadium Disaster - A dark day in Liverpool's history

One of the darkest days in Liverpool FC's history, the Heysel Stadium disaster in 1985.

2005

Istanbul miracle

A day heralded as ‘The Miracle of Istanbul’, and a day where Liverpool completed the “greatest comeback in sport” to secure their fifth European Cup.

2015

The day the ‘Normal One’ arrived to set Liverpool on course back to their perch

After years of uncertainty and a lack of clear long-term direction, the arrival of Jurgen Klopp flipped Liverpool on its head and returned the club back to its rightful place.

2019

‘Lets talk about six, baby!’ – Liverpool break Klopp’s duck with European Cup

The final was anything but pretty, but Liverpool’s exponential rise under Jurgen Klopp was rewarded with its first piece of silverware in the shape of Ol’ Big Ears.

2020

Liverpool FC win the Premier League

After 11,016 days, Liverpool Football Club are the champions of England for the 19th time, ending a 30-year title drought.
